Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel my membership anytime? +

Yes. All memberships are month-to-month with no long-term contracts. Cancel online or by contacting us — takes effect at the end of your billing cycle.

Do unused credits roll over? +

Yes, unused monthly service credits roll over for up to 3 months. After that, they expire. Discount perks (% off) are always available as long as your membership is active.

Can I share my membership benefits? +

Memberships are individual and tied to one person. However, you can gift unused credits to friends or family — just let us know at the front desk.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan? +

Absolutely. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle. Any unused credits from your current plan transfer with you.
